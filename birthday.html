💛 reasons you're actually amazing

Why You're My Favorite
( I'll never say this out loud, obviously )

Haan haan, main jaanta hoon main itna funny nahi hoon. But jab tum hasti ho — genuinely hasti ho — toh sab kuch worth it lagta hai. Don't tell anyone I said this.
Tum care karte ho. Bahut zyada. And you try to hide it with attitude, but I see it. Apne log ke liye tum mountains move kar do — quietly. That's rare.
Jab koi tumhare paas aata hai kisi baat ke liye — tum actually sunti ho. Poori tarah. No phone, no distraction. That's a superpower and you use it beautifully.
Koi nahi samajhta tumhare jokes. Lekin tum khud hasti raho unpe. Mujhe woh baat pasand hai. It's completely unhinged and I love it.
Bad day? Better with you. Good day? Even better. Random Tuesday? Somehow becomes a memory. Yeh koi spell hai kya? Seriously, how.
📬 A Properly Written Letter (for once)

Okay. Setting the jokes aside for exactly one paragraph. Maybe two.

Tum jo bhi ho — chaotic, dramatic, endlessly stubborn, fries-stealing, hamesha kalesh karne wali — I genuinely wouldn't trade any of it. Every weird, annoying, wonderful thing about you is the reason I keep showing up.

Tumhare saath sab kuch thoda zyada bright lagta hai. Bad days feel manageable. Good days feel like memories worth keeping. Aur ordinary moments — chai, vadapav, late night talks, — become the best parts of any day.

Aaj tumhara birthday hai. Aur main chahta hoon ki tum jaano — not just today — ki tum genuinely kisi ke liye matter karti ho. Bahut zyada.

Happy Birthday, Nena. Khub saari khushiyan aayein. Aur please — kalesh karna band karo. At least today. As a gift to me.
